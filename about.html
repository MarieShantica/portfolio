I'm Marie Wibisana, a 19-year-old creative from Indonesia. Upon self-teaching myself digital painting at fifteen, I got enthralled by how technology and art could intersect through design, which brought me to pursue an Honors Program degree in Computational Media (with People and Interaction Design threads) and a minor in Industrial Design at Georgia Tech.

I love wandering upon the realms of UI/UX design, graphic design, frontend development, and digital marketing. To me, design isn't simply making things look pretty, but rather an optimization to one's daily life, a potential mood booster, and a means of sharing personal stories. My designs range from goofy animation reels on YouTube to wireframes for a potential app - either way, I find true joy in crafting digital creations that draw smiles on people around me.

In my free time, I draw, vlog, play the ukulele, sing, write poems... and am a hopeful romantic.
